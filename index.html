About This Hub

This is an independent, fan-made project — not affiliated with or endorsed by KRAFTON. It exists because BGMI's mechanics deserve a clear, no-fluff explanation, and the Indian community deserves a space built specifically for it.

Every breakdown here is written from actual matches, patch notes, and testing — not recycled from other sites. The goal is simple: help players understand why something works, not just repeat that it does.

Long term, this hub is built to grow alongside the game — matching new modes, maps, and metas as they land.
